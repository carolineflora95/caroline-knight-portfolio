Vanilla Weather Application

A simple, user-friendly weather application showing the current and future weather for over a million locations around the world. There is an integrated geo-location feature, as well as wind speed, humidity, and real feel data. The app combines HTML, CSS, and Vanilla Javascript, with help from Bootstrap and Google Fonts in the styling.
